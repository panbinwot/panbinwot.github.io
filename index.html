Who Am I?

Hi I'm Bin Pan (Mandarin: 潘斌). I'm not really good at introducing myself. So, here are some random facts about Bin.

Bin is a passionate young man from Beijing, China. He studied accounting at a local business-like college in Beijing. After 4 years of struggling with accounting and law terms, he finally gave up his career in accounting and turned to data science.

He always pictures himself at the intersection of finance, statistical learning, and product development. Well, therapist says that being confident is good for him. But the truth is he is not good at any of these. He’s also proficient in food poisoning himself.

Also, he prefers HIMYM than Friends, though he dreamed of RR’s love story all the time. He met some girl in NYC. But what he thought was there, she thought differently. Yea, he wasn’t hurt at all.
